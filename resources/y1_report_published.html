Year 1 Collaboration Report Published

CAPIBARA celebrates its first birthday reflecting on enormous progress and looking forward to next steps.

Y1 Collaboration Status Report Cover

The CAPIBARA team is excited to announce the publication of its Year 1 Status Report in which we cover the following aspects:

  • Progress in the first year: How we put an idea to paper while building valuable partnerships
  • CAPICR: Next tasks to bring our cosmic ray detector to reality
  • CAPIGX: Our long-term vision for a high-energy monitoring mission, with upcoming paper with details
Read the report here !

Alongside the report, the CAPIBARA Collaboration also published a Call for New Members, read more on the detailed release.

"This breakthrough in detector technology will significantly enhance our ability to study gamma-ray bursts and active galactic nuclei. The improved sensitivity could help us detect previously unseen phenomena in the high-energy universe."
