Exploring the High-Energy Universe

CAPIBARA is an international student collaboration learning about gamma-ray bursts and cosmic rays as well as designing and building instruments to detect those.

Join Us

Two Missions, One Vision

CRD
COSMIC RAY DETECTOR

A student-built instrument integrated into the OBA FARADAY CubeSat. Flying in LEO to detect solar and extra-solar primary cosmic rays, using Time-of-Flight techniques to distinguish their origins.

Learn about CRD
GAMMA
Gamma-ray Analysis for Multi-Messenger Astrophysics

A mission concept to observe gamma-ray bursts and advacing in our understanding of fundamental physics.

Explore GAMMA
