Why Join Us?

The CAPIBARA Collaborations is ever growing and we'd like to have you along us! Beyond hardware and science, CAPIBARA is about building community of the new generation of space experts. We're creating a collaboration where students don't just learn about space, they actually engage with it.

For Students

Work on real flight hardware and software, with simulations and operations. CAPIABRA is not only engineering, but also science, outreach, communication... there's a place for you to contribute. No prior experience required, we are here to learn! Application Form

For Advisors

Mentor the next generation. Join COSMOS' Advisory Board or contact the CRD team. You provide invaluable knowledge and guidance! Contact Us!

For Institutions

Partner with us, provide lab space, expertise, funding or host a student team. Help scale this student-centred mission model. Contact for partnership
