Email for Foundations#

Subject: Funding Support Request for CAPIBARA Student Space Research Mission

Dear [Name],

I am reaching out on behalf of CAPIBARA, a student-led space research initiative formed by a highly motivated group of high school and university students. Our goal is to launch a cosmic-ray-detecting instrument into space!

To achieve this, we have applied to the Spark Program by the Spanish company PLD Space and are working with OBA Space, who will host our instrument aboard their OBA FARADAY satellite.

Although our team has a strong background in research and engineering, thanks to participation in programs like Youth and Science, our biggest obstacle is funding. The Spark Program provides free launch services, but we lack the financial resources for manufacturing, testing, and integrating the payload. We are all volunteers and currently operate with no external funding.

We are actively forming partnerships with companies, schools, research institutions, and universities for technical assistance and mentoring. However, their ability to offer financial support is uncertain.

The [Foundation Name] Foundation has long [recognized/contributed to/supported…] — [connect to their mission and values]. We believe our goals are aligned with your commitment to [science/education/youth empowerment/etc.].

This is a truly ambitious mission. It highlights the capabilities of students to engage in serious scientific endeavors, while also promoting STEAM education and space exploration at the secondary and tertiary levels. Your support would not only make this project possible but would also inspire a new generation of scientists and engineers. Naturally, you would receive full recognition for your support.

Please don’t hesitate to contact us with any questions or ideas. We are eager to discuss how we can collaborate. Feel free to share this information with your colleagues.

You can find more information about our mission, technical plans, and team on our website: capibara3.github.io, where you can also schedule a meeting.

Sincerely, [Your Name] [Your Role in CAPIBARA]
