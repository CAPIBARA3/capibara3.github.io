About#

Structure#

Here’s to the structure of the CAPIBARA Collaboration:

  • Parent organisation: The CAPIBARA Collaboration

    • CAPIBARA-CRD: Cosmic Ray Detector Pathfinder. Led by Lluc Soler (in collaboration with OBA Space FARADAY and its partners, and PLD Space)

    • CAPIBARA-COSMOS: High-energy astrophysics program. Led by Joan Alcaide Núñez

GitHub naming strategy and organisation:#

The CAPIBARA Collaboration organisation (github.com/capibara3) is sub-divided into the CAPIBARA-CRD and CAPIBARA-COSMOS teams. Each team has it’s own discussions, repositories, and projects which are named following “crd-name” or “cosmos-name”. There are some collaboration-wide repositories (.github and .github-private) and some discussions as well for collaboration administration.

Who to reach out to?#

Most of the contacts are either mentioned on a project’s page in the main website or can be found in the team section. Besides, most collaboration internal communications happens on GitHub discussions conversations insde of issues and PRs.

Anyways, here is a brief summary of important contacts:

  • Main contact site for any enquiry to the collaboration lead

  • Official email is linked to the contact form. Members of the collaboration may request the direct address contacting us.

  • For any collaboration-wide or CAPIGX related messages you may contact @joanalnu

  • For any technical CAPICR messages you may contact @luke3773
