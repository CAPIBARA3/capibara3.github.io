## Overview

The CAPIBARA Collaboration is a volunteer-based international student research collective focused on space science and high-energy astrophysics instrumentation. Our governance is designed to be transparent, fair, and educational while producing high-quality scientific outputs.


## Core Principles

1. **Open Science:** Default to open licensing and public dissemination
2. **Student-Led:** Empower students in leadership and decision-making
3. **Transparency:** Document decisions and maintain public records
4. **Safety First:** Protect all members, especially minors
5. **Merit-Based:** Recognize contributions fairly and objectively
