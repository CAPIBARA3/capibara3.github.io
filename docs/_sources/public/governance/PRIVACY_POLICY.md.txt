# CAPIBARA Collaboration Communications & Data Policy
## Version 1.0 | Effective Date: January 1, 2026

### 1. Communication Channels Hierarchy

#### A. Official Channels (All members must join)
1.  **GitHub Organization** (`CAPIBARA3`)
    - Source of truth for all technical documentation
    - Issues & Projects for task tracking
    - Discussions for Q&A
2. **Official Email**
2.  **Email Lists**
    - `capibaracollaboration@outlook.com`
    - `crd.capibara@outlook.com`
    - `cosmos.capibara@outlook.com`
3.  **Monthly All-Hands Video Call**
    - Agree to a date
    - Notes posted on GitHub docs repository

#### B. Informal Channels (Optional)
- WhatsApp: the Whatsapp group is voluntary and not managed by the CAPIBARA Collaboration.


### 2. Data Privacy & Security

- We collect only: Name, email, institution, role, GitHub metadata (by joining the collaboration)
- Stored in our GitHub website repository. Storage of data is voluntary, except GitHub username.
- Right to be forgotten: Email the general collaboration email to request your personal data deletion anytime.
- We never share your personal data without your consent


### 3. External Communications

#### A. Social Media Guidelines
- **Official accounts:** Managed by Communications Lead
- **Individual members:** May share public info with disclaimer: "Views my own"
- **Never share:** Unreleased data, internal disputes, proprietary designs

### 4. Internal Transparency Requirements
- Partnership decisions: Announced within 24 hours
- Technical failures: Post-mortem within 1 week
- Meeting minutes: Posted within 48 hours

### 5. Violation Procedure
1. First offense: Private warning from Program Director
2. Second offense: Temporary suspension (2 weeks)
3. Third offense: Membership review (see MEMBERSHIP_POLICY.md)
