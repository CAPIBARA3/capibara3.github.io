# CAPIBARA Collaboration Authorship & Publication Policy
## Version 1.0 | Effective Date: January 1, 2026

## 1. Publication Categories

### A. Technical/Instrument Papers
(e.g., "Design of the CAPIBARA-1 GAGG Detector")
**Author list:** All contributors to the specific work described, listed alphabetically.
**Collaboration author:** "on behalf of the CAPIBARA Collaboration"

### B. Science Results Papers  
(e.g., "First GRB detection with CAPIBARA-1")
**Author list:** The CAPIBARA Collaboration (with consortium author list in appendix)
**Writing committee:** 5-7 members who drafted the paper, listed first alphabetically

### C. Student Theses
Students may use collaboration data for theses with approval from their Program Director.

## 2. Authorship Requirements
To be listed as author on collaboration papers, one must:
1. Make significant contribution to instrumentation, software, data analysis, or interpretation
2. Participate in paper review
3. Approve final manuscript

## 3. Dispute Resolution
Authorship disputes go to Advisory Board for mediation.
