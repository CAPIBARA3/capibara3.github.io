Before You Apply

Welcome! We're excited that you want to join us. Here's what you need to know:

1

Read the Guidelines

To inform you and avoid misunderstandings we crafted an application guideline you can access here. This page contains all the information you need to know joining the CAPIBARA Collaboration.

2

Fill the Form

Complete the application form below. It takes about 5 minutes. After submissions, we'll get in touch via email within a few days.

3

Submit Documents

Once accepted, you'll need to send: (1) signed membership agreement, and (2) optional supporting docs (CV, motivation letter, etc.).

Don't let bureaucracy stop you! We welcome all students, including high school and undergraduate students. We're here to support you every step of the way.
