Some Advice Before Applying to Join

We welcome new collaborators from all backgrounds: professionals, students, researchers, and enthusiasts. Please, fill out the form below to get in touch with us. We will try to get back to you as soon as possible.

You can read the latest call for new members and the official guideline to the application. We encourage you to read the latter document throughout before contacting us.

As stated in the mentioned guideline, students wanting to become full members of the CAPIBARA Collaboration need to provide the following documents in PDF format: (1) signed membership agreement (please the document carefully), (2) any supporting documentation you want to share with us like an academic curriculum vitae, motivation letter, research interest statement, links to coding projects or previous, highlighted papers or reports. The second point is optional and won't affect in any way your application, read our selection requirements in the previously mentioned guideline. The mentioned documents are to be sent after you hear from us and via email to the official collaboration-wide address.

Please, don't let any of these bureaucratic steps prevent you from joining us. The CAPIBARA Collaboration is open to all students and we will be happy to support you and answer your questions.

Additional requirements and documents are also required for institutional partnerships, collaborations and potentially advising. Let us get back to you in the first place and we will discuss this matter.
