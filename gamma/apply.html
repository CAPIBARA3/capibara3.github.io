What We're Currently Looking For

GAMMA is in Phase 1. We need people to build the science foundation. Priority roles right now:

GRB Astrophysics

Population statistics, spectral modelling, science case writing.

ML & Data Analysis

Transient classification, archival data analysis (Fermi, Swift).

Theoretical Physics

Fundamental physics tests, cosmological applications of GRBs.

Aerospace / Instrumentation

CubeSat systems, detector design, mission architecture.

Not sure where you fit? Tell us your interests and we'll find a home for you.
